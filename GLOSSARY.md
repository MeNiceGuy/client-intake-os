# Glossary

## Client Facing
Outputs suitable to share directly with the client.

## Internal
Consultant-only notes used for fit, service logic, and decision support.

## Proof / Intake Record
The raw or semi-structured record of what was said, observed, or captured during intake.

## Fit Assessment
A judgment about whether the prospect and the likely work are a good match.

## Service Alignment
A recommendation about what type of help appears to fit best.

## Evidence Boundary
A reminder not to overclaim what the intake can prove.
