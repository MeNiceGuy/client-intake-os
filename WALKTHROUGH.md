# Walkthrough

## Step 1
Review the redacted sample intake packet.

## Step 2
Inspect the templates and decide what intake artifacts you want to use.

## Step 3
Capture discovery notes, stated goals, and pain points.

## Step 4
Produce:
- discovery summary
- fit assessment
- service alignment
- next-step recommendation
- follow-up summary

## Step 5
Use `QUALITY_CHECKLIST.md` and `HOW_TO_EVALUATE_OUTPUT.md` to judge whether the intake is good enough.
