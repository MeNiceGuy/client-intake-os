I built and packaged a Client Intake OS focused on structuring discovery, qualification, and next-step recommendations in a cleaner way.

The goal was to create something stronger than vague intake notes, generic discovery forms, or loose qualification thinking.

This workflow is designed to support:
- client discovery
- pain-point capture
- fit assessment
- service alignment
- next-step recommendation
- follow-up preparation

I documented it as a GitHub project to show how I think about consulting systems, workflow design, and better decision support.
