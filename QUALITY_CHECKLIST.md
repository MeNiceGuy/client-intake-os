# Quality Checklist

Use this checklist to judge whether an intake generated from this system is strong enough.

## Discovery quality
- Is client context captured clearly?
- Are the stated goals and pain points specific?
- Are assumptions separated from what the client actually said?

## Qualification quality
- Is the fit assessment clear?
- Is there enough information to recommend a next step honestly?
- Are risks or caution flags noted?

## Output quality
- Is the summary readable?
- Does the intake avoid generic consultant filler?
- Would this help a real consulting conversation move forward?
