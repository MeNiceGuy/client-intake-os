# Adapt This To Your Process

This project is intentionally flexible. It is designed to help you structure intake better, not force every consulting workflow into the same shape.

## Things you may want to adapt
- the discovery questions you ask
- the depth of qualification
- the exact fit categories you use
- the service alignment logic
- the tone of your client-facing summaries

## Good adaptation rule
Keep the separation between:
- what the client actually said
- what you infer
- what you recommend next

That separation is one of the main strengths of the system.
