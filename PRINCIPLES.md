# Principles

## 1. Discovery before prescription
Do not recommend too much before the problem is clear.

## 2. Separate what the client said from what you infer
That distinction improves trust and decision quality.

## 3. Keep client-facing outputs cleaner than raw intake notes
Good consulting requires better organization than a call transcript.

## 4. Fit matters
Not every prospect is the right fit for every service path.

## 5. Practical beats clever
The point is to improve decisions, not to sound impressive.
