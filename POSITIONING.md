# Positioning

## Best public framing

Client Intake OS is best framed as:
- a consulting intake workflow system
- a structured discovery and qualification toolkit
- a practical client intake and next-step framework

## Avoid framing it as

- a magical sales closer
- a CRM replacement for every use case
- an AI system that understands the client better than the client does

## Strong positioning themes

- structured intake workflow
- discovery clarity
- qualification logic
- service-fit thinking
- cleaner next-step recommendations
