# Quickstart

## Goal
Create a structured consulting intake workflow that produces cleaner discovery notes, qualification thinking, and next-step recommendations.

## High-level flow
1. capture client and business context
2. log stated goals and pain points
3. separate client statements from consultant interpretation
4. assess fit and likely next step
5. produce client-facing and internal outputs

## Best starting point
Review the redacted sample intake packet first, then inspect the templates.
